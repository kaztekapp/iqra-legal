IQra2.0

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your IQra2.0 account and associated data, follow these steps:

  • Open the IQra2.0 app
  • Go to the Profile tab
  • Tap Log Out to sign out of your account
  • To permanently delete your account and all associated data, send an email to admin@mkaztek.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and authentication credentials
  • Your display name and profile information
  • Your learning progress, XP, streaks, and achievements

Data Retention

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. After deletion, your data cannot be recovered.

Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained as it cannot be linked back to individual users.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at admin@mkaztek.com.
